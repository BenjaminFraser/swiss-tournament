Welcome

The Puppy Haven is a reliable, responsible and moderated interface through which young puppies are registered, tracked and given new homes to verified shelters and responsible owners. You can browse through our shelter list at the shelter page, where you'll find the current puppies within that shelter, along with location and contact details.

By registering for an account and applying for validation, you can be on track to adopting your own puppy through our system. Our system also facilitates checking your own animals into our shelters for periods of time if you require, and provided the shelter has the capacity to do so.

This platform was build by Benjamin D Fraser using the Flask framework, in conjunction with SQLAlchemy, SQLite, Bootstrap, custom HTML/CSS, JavaScript and Python. Form validation was built using WTForms within Flask..
